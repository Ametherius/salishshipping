What We Do

– Salish Shipping Co helps individuals and businesses ship boxes, parcels, envelopes, cartons, pails, small containers and tubes via courier from Canadian addresses all over the world. We also provide quotes for palletized shipments within North America.

– Thanks to our extensive network, clients can expect to pay 20% cheaper than booking couriers directly or using Canada Post. International shipments and multi-box shipments receive even higher discounts.

– The typical shipment has numerous quoted speed options. Some are as fast as 2 business days across the continent and 3 to overseas destinations.

We must all work together towards reconciliation for a better Canada.

The Salish Sea has been an integral part of my life for 33 years and will continue to be forever. My home, Salt Spring Island, is ancestral and unceded traditional territory of the Hul’q’umi’num and SENĆOŦEN speaking peoples. Most land where we ship to and ship from is also unceded traditional territory of various indigenous peoples. By recognizing these realities, and sharing this recognition, my goal is to give respect where respect is due.

-Gabriel McPhee
